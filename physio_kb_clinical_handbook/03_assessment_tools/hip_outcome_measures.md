# Hip Outcome Measures

## Good core set
- numeric pain rating scale
- PSFS
- HOOS or HOOS JR where appropriate
- iHOT-12 for younger active populations
- LEFS when a broader lower-limb measure is preferable

## Useful context measures
- walking tolerance
- sit duration tolerance
- side-lying tolerance for lateral hip pain
- step count or return-to-run markers when relevant

## Practical tip
Use one region-specific scale plus PSFS and one clearly documented comparable task.
