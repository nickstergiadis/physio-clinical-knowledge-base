# Case-Mix Review and Coverage Update

## What this cleanup pass addressed
This build was reviewed against your actual case mix and the gaps from earlier versions were closed where they were clearly recurring or high-value.

## Covered clusters
- **Lumbar spine:** non-specific low back pain, lumbar radicular pain / sciatica, lumbar assessment, outcome measures, and rehab progression
- **Hip / lateral hip:** hip OA, FAIS, GTPS, hip assessment, hip progression, and hip post-op annexes
- **Knee:** knee OA, PFP, degenerative meniscal tear, post-op ACLR + meniscal repair, TKA annex
- **Ankle / foot:** lateral ankle sprain, chronic ankle instability, midportion Achilles tendinopathy, Achilles / gastroc post-op annexes
- **Shoulder:** RCRSP, adhesive capsulitis, anterior instability, shoulder post-op annexes
- **Cervical:** mechanical neck pain and cervicogenic headache plus cervical assessment and progression
- **Elbow / forearm:** lateral / medial elbow tendinopathy plus cubital tunnel and radial tunnel / PIN differential support
- **Hand / wrist:** De Quervain and load-related wrist/hand tendinopathy with dedicated assessment and progression
- **TMJ:** myogenous TMD and mobility / disc-dominant TMJ presentations

## Practical notes
- the package is now organized as a working clinical reference rather than a series of disconnected topic drops
- clusters are more structurally consistent, though post-op content remains annex-based rather than a rigid protocol manual
- evidence pages are intended as review prompts and update targets, not a substitute for re-checking current literature or surgeon instructions
