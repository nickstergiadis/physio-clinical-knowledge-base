# Cervical Rehab Progression Framework

## Phase 1: calm / restore movement confidence
- comfortable ROM
- deep neck flexor support work if tolerated
- thoracic mobility
- breathing and general activity
- symptom-guided exposure to previously avoided movement

## Phase 2: rebuild endurance and control
- cervical flexor / extensor endurance
- scapular support strength
- graded rotation / extension exposure
- posture tolerance through capacity building rather than rigid “correction”

## Phase 3: return to demand
- lifting, carries, pressing, and pulling progressions as appropriate
- workstation and task-specific exposure
- flare-up management plan
