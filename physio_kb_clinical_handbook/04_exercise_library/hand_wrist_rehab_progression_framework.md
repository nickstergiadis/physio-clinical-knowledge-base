# Hand / Wrist Rehab Progression Framework

## Phase 1: calm / protect
- reduce high-irritability thumb and wrist exposure temporarily
- splinting only when clearly helpful
- comfortable ROM and tendon gliding where appropriate
- short bouts of low-irritability isometrics if tolerated

## Phase 2: rebuild
- graded wrist and thumb loading
- grip and pinch capacity
- forearm strength and endurance
- task pacing and progressive re-exposure

## Phase 3: return to demand
- heavier carries, gripping, lifting, sport-specific or work-specific tasks
- flare-up plan for repetitive load spikes
