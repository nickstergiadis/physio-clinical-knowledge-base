# Cervical Cluster Evidence Update

## Main themes to re-check during updates
- exercise remains central for mechanical neck pain
- manual therapy may help short term but should not replace a loading / self-management plan
- headache care should distinguish cervicogenic pattern from migraine or other primary headache disorders
- persistent arm symptoms require updated neural / imaging referral thresholds based on presentation

## Suggested update targets
- mechanical neck pain CPGs
- cervicogenic headache systematic reviews
- upper-quarter neural / radicular guidance
