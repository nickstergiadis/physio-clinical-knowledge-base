# Hand / Wrist Cluster Evidence Update

## Main themes to re-check during updates
- De Quervain care commonly combines activity modification, splinting, exercise, and injection depending on irritability and goals
- exercise evidence for broader hand/wrist tendinopathy is still relatively limited compared with larger joints
- thumb CMC and neural differentials should be reviewed when response is incomplete

## Suggested update targets
- De Quervain treatment reviews
- hand/wrist tendinopathy rehab reviews
- thumb CMC and peripheral nerve differential resources
