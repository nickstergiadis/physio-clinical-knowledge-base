# Hip Cluster Evidence Update

## Main themes to re-check during updates
- exercise and education remain the core of most non-operative hip management
- imaging should support clinical reasoning, not replace it
- lateral hip pain care should account for both tendon compression and load-capacity issues
- arthroscopy decisions in FAIS should be based on symptoms, imaging, goals, and response to conservative care rather than imaging alone
- arthroplasty and repair rehab still require surgeon-specific reconciliation

## Suggested update targets
- hip OA guideline updates
- FAIS consensus / surgery vs conservative care reviews
- GTPS diagnostic and exercise-treatment reviews
- return-to-sport guidance after hip arthroscopy
