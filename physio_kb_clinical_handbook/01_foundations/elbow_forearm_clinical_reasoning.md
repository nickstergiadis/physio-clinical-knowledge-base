# Elbow / Forearm Clinical Reasoning

## Main buckets
- lateral elbow tendinopathy
- medial elbow tendinopathy
- ulnar neuropathy / cubital tunnel
- radial tunnel / PIN pattern
- cervical referral or peripheral neural contribution
- intra-articular / instability / fracture or inflammatory drivers when indicated

## Useful discriminators
- local tendon pain with gripping vs neural symptoms with numbness, burning, paresthesia, or weakness
- symptom distribution
- night symptoms or prolonged flexion intolerance
- response to resisted wrist tests vs neural provocation

## Practical management logic
- identify tendon-dominant vs neural-dominant vs mixed
- load management and progressive resistance are usually central for tendon presentations
- persistent weakness or sensory change should trigger a clearer neurologic workup
