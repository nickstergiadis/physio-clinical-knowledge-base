# Hand / Wrist Clinical Reasoning

## Main buckets
- De Quervain tenosynovitis
- load-related tendon pain around wrist / thumb
- CMC irritation
- median / ulnar / radial neural contribution
- inflammatory or traumatic driver
- cervical or proximal referral when pattern is atypical

## Practical logic
- define the exact pain region and task triggers
- separate tendon pain from joint irritability and neural symptoms
- use splinting and load modification selectively while rebuilding capacity
