# TMJ Clinical Reasoning

## Main buckets
- myogenous TMD
- mobility / disc-dominant disorder
- trauma / acute lock / fracture concern
- dental / sinus / ENT / neural mimics when indicated
- cervical contribution or coexistence

## Useful discriminators
- jaw opening capacity and deviation pattern
- clicking / locking history
- chew, clench, yawn, and morning symptoms
- headache / neck pain overlap
- psychosocial and parafunctional drivers

## Practical management logic
- use conservative reversible care first in most routine cases
- separate painful movement limitation from true locking
- coordinate with dental / medical colleagues when presentation is not straightforward
