# Shoulder Bankart / Arthroscopic Stabilization Post-Op Notes

## Use this annex for
- arthroscopic Bankart / stabilization rehab planning for traumatic anterior instability

## Non-negotiable note
Always reconcile against:
- first-time vs recurrent instability history
- Bankart-only vs remplissage / other augmentation
- bone loss / lesion burden
- surgeon restrictions

## Practical rehab frame
### Protection phase
- sling / immobilization as directed
- hand, wrist, elbow mobility
- early protected shoulder movement only within surgeon limits

### Early motion phase
- cautious restoration of elevation and rotation
- protect anterior structures
- avoid aggressive abduction + external rotation early

### Strength / control phase
- scapular control
- rotator cuff and kinetic-chain strengthening
- proprioception and perturbation work

### Return phase
- sport- or work-specific progression
- build confidence and recurrence-risk awareness
- use criteria-based progression, not time alone

## Evidence update note
The current BESS-commissioned evidence synthesis identifies 10 postop rehab domains and recommends early movement during the immobilization period, but only within constrained ranges (elevation to 90 degrees, anterior to the scapular plane, neutral ER).
